The Training Partnership

Meet The McEvoy Team

Tony and Calvin McEvoy. Group 1 Cups Kings. Hall of Fame. Training partnership.

Tony McEvoy is one of Australia's most decorated thoroughbred trainers. A Hall of Fame inductee with Group 1 victories at the Melbourne Cup, Caulfield Cup, and every major carnival on the Australian racing calendar. Operating dual stables at Morphettville and Flemington, his patient, horse-first approach has produced over 750 winners across every state.

Calvin McEvoy brings a fresh perspective to the partnership, combining modern race-day analysis with instincts shaped by a lifetime inside the stable. Together they are one of the most formidable training combinations in the country.
